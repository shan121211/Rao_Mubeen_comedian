About Rao Mubeen

Rao Mubeen is a passionate Standup Comedian Pakistan who has been actively writing and performing comedy since 2019. Known for his sharp observations, relatable storytelling, and unique desi perspective, he has built a reputation among audiences looking for quality Pakistani Standup Comedy.

Over the years, Rao Mubeen has performed at some of Pakistan’s leading universities and Punjabi pakistani comedy. His comedy explores everyday life, cultural differences, social behavior, relationships, and hilarious real-life experiences that resonate with audiences of all ages.

From packed university auditoriums to corporate gatherings and public events, he continues to deliver memorable performances while sharing stages with some of Pakistan’s most recognized comedians and entertainers He Performed in Pakistani Popular Abbas bukhari comedy show.
